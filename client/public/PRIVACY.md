# Privacy

void-cast is a public, real-time space.
It does not require accounts or collect personal information such as names or emails.

---

## What is stored

* Messages (“casts”) submitted by users
* Timestamps associated with those messages

All messages are public and may be visible to others.

---

## What is not collected

* No user accounts
* No profiles
* No direct personal identifiers are required

---

## Technical data

Basic technical data (such as IP addresses) may be processed by the server for:

* operating the service
* preventing abuse
* ensuring stability

This data is not used for tracking or profiling.

---

## Important

Do not share personal, sensitive, or confidential information.
Anything you submit may be visible to others.

---

## Data retention

Content is temporary and may be removed at any time.
There is no guarantee of storage or persistence.

---

## Changes

This policy may be updated as the project evolves.
